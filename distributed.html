Distributed Blockchain


The blockchain is distributed among all peers who are part of the blockchain system. Each individual holds a copy of the blockchain, also called a ledger. Thus compromising such a system would require compromise of atleast 50% of all the systems that are part of the system. This essentially gives the blockchain its immuatable nature.
